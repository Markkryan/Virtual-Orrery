Commands:
-----------------------------------------
$make: creates ./Boilerplate.out
$make clean: removes ./Boilerplate.out
$./Boilerplate.out: Runs program



Program Controls:
-----------------------------------------

-Click and drag the mouse to rotate the camera
-Scroll will zoom in/out from the center of scene
-Spacebar: pauses the animation
-R: Resets the orrey
